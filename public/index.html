NBA Auction

Draft a 5-man roster (2 Guards, 2 Forwards, 1 Center) with a $20 budget against a bot rival. Real NBA players come up one at a time, in an order only the game knows — bid or pass before the next name drops. Rosters are optimized into position slots; playing someone out of position costs a bit of value. Final scores are shown as a Talent Score and team shape, not raw numbers.
